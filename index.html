Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can you close?
Depending on your situation, we can often close in as few as 5 days. Because we buy with cash, there's no waiting on bank financing. If you need more time, we'll close on whatever date works best for you.
Do I need to make repairs or clean up?
Not at all. We buy houses completely as-is. Leave behind anything you don't want, skip the repairs, and don't worry about cleaning — we take care of everything after closing.
Are there any fees or commissions?
No. Unlike a traditional listing, there are no agent commissions and no hidden fees. The offer we make is the amount you receive at closing, with no surprises.
Is my offer really no-obligation?
Absolutely. Requesting an offer is completely free with zero obligation. Take your time to review it, ask questions, and decide what's right for you and your family. There's never any pressure.
Can you help if I'm behind on payments or in foreclosure?
Yes — this is one of our specialties. Depending on the circumstances, we may be able to take over your payments and help you avoid foreclosure. The sooner you reach out, the more options we'll have to help.
