Common questions

How do you decide on your offer?
We look at your home's location, condition, needed repairs, and recent sales of comparable properties nearby. We'll always explain how we reached the number so it makes sense to you.
Is there any cost to get an offer?
None at all. Requesting an offer is 100% free and there's zero obligation to accept.
What if my house needs a lot of work?
That's exactly what we do. We buy homes in any condition — including those needing major repairs — so you don't have to spend a dime fixing anything.
How fast can I close?
In many cases, as few as 5 days. Because we buy with cash, there's no waiting on a bank. If you need more time, we'll close whenever works best for you.
