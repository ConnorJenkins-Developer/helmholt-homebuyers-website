Our story

Real people, real solutions

When time is not on your side, Helmholt HomeBuyers Inc. is the go-to resource to quickly resolve any and all of your real estate needs. Founded in 2009 by Jason and Carrie Helmholt, our company has become the real estate solution for so many of today's homeowners by understanding and addressing their most pressing needs.

Specializing in pre-foreclosures, short sales, bankruptcies, rental properties, and estate sales, we're well-regarded as a premier authority for these transactions throughout the St. Louis metropolitan area and surrounding counties.

Our motivated team is experienced in handling today's constantly changing situations. Whether you're dealing with a problem tenant, needing to sell a home quickly due to divorce, bankruptcy, or a move out of town — we can help. Depending on the circumstances, we may even be able to take over your payments, often closing within 5 days.
